<p style={{ fontSize: '1.25rem', fontWeight: 700, marginBottom: '0.75rem' }}>Overview</p>

Polystack Disaster Recovery protects workloads from unplanned outages through
continuous replication, automated failover, and orchestrated recovery. This guide
covers creating protection plans, executing failover and failback procedures, running
DR tests to validate recovery objectives, and troubleshooting common issues.
