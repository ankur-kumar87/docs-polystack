<p style={{ fontSize: '1.25rem', fontWeight: 700, marginBottom: '0.75rem' }}>Overview</p>

The XDR Administration Guide covers the full administrative lifecycle of Polystack Disaster
Recovery — from initial site registration and replication topology setup through ongoing
operational management, compliance reporting, and security hardening.

<AdminWarning />

<Note>
  **Prerequisites**
  - Administrator credentials with the `admin` role on both primary and DR sites
  - Access to **XDeploy** (`https://connect.<your-domain>`) on both sites
  - Network connectivity established between primary and DR sites (dedicated replication link recommended)
  - Familiarity with RPO/RTO concepts and distributed storage replication
</Note>
