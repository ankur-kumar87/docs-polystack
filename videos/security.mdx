## Set Up Two-Factor Authentication

Protect your Xloud account with Two-Factor Authentication from User Center → Security. Scan the QR in any authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, Aegis), verify the 6-digit code, and save the 10 one-time recovery codes — every sign-in from then on needs a code from your phone.
