## Manage DNS Zones and Record Sets

Create a DNS zone (primary or secondary) with a name, email, and TTL, then add record sets. Supported record types include A, AAAA, CAA, CNAME, MX, NS, PTR, SOA, SPF, SRV, SSHFP, and TXT.
