## Back Up and Restore a Volume

## Recover Files from a Snapshot

Pull individual files out of a volume or instance snapshot — no full-volume restore needed. Open the snapshot's filesystem in the Dashboard, navigate folders, and download a single file or a ZIP of selected files.
